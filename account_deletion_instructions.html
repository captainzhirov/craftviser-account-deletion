How to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps to permanently delete your Craftviser account:

Step 1: Open Your Profile

  1. Open the Craftviser app on your device
  2. Tap on the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. You should see your profile information displayed

Step 2: Access Account Settings

  1. In your profile screen, look for the Settings or Edit Profile option
  2. Tap on it to access your account settings
  3. Scroll down to find account management options

Step 3: Find Delete Account Option

  1. Look for "Delete Account" or "Remove Account" option
  2. This option is usually located at the bottom of the settings menu
  3. It may be in a red-colored section to indicate it's a destructive action

Step 4: Confirm Deletion

  1. Tap on the "Delete Account" option
  2. You will see a confirmation dialog warning you about permanent deletion
  3. Read the warning carefully
  4. Type "DELETE" or confirm your decision as prompted
  5. Tap "Confirm Delete" or "Yes, Delete My Account"

Step 5: Account Deletion Process

  1. After confirmation, your account deletion will begin
  2. This process may take a few moments to complete
  3. You will be automatically logged out of the app
  4. You will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Immediate: You will be logged out of the app and cannot log back in
  • Within 24 hours: All your data is permanently removed from our servers
  • Email confirmation: You'll receive an email confirming the deletion
  • App access: You can create a new account anytime if you change your mind

Frequently Asked Questions

A: No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be permanently removed.

Q: How long does the deletion process take?

A: The deletion process begins immediately and all data is removed within 24 hours.

Q: Can I create a new account with the same email?

A: Yes, you can create a new account with the same email address after your previous account is deleted.

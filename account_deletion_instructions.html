Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers, including:

Please make sure you want to permanently delete your account before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps to permanently delete your Craftviser account:

Step 1: Open Your Profile

  1. Open the Craftviser app on your device
  2. Tap on the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. You will see your profile information with your name, username, and activity statistics

Step 2: Access Edit Profile

  1. In your profile screen, look for the Edit Profile button
  2. Tap on "Edit Profile" to access your account settings
  3. You will be taken to the Edit Profile screen where you can modify your account details

Step 3: Find Delete Account Option

  1. Scroll down to the bottom of the Edit Profile screen
  2. Look for the red "Delete Account" button
  3. The button will have a trash can icon and the text "Permanently delete your account"
  4. This option is located below the "Sign Out" button

Step 4: Confirm Deletion

  1. Tap on the red "Delete Account" button
  2. A dialog box will appear with the title "Delete Account"
  3. The dialog will ask: "Are you sure you want to delete your account?"
  4. Below that, you'll see: "This action cannot be undone."
  5. Tap the red "Confirm" button to proceed

Step 5: Account Deletion Process

  1. After tapping "Confirm", the deletion process will begin immediately
  2. You'll see a loading indicator while the process completes
  3. The process typically takes 10-30 seconds
  4. Once complete, you'll be automatically signed out and redirected to the Welcome screen
  5. A green success message will appear: "Account deleted successfully."
  6. All your data (activities, goals, folders, friends) will be permanently removed

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Immediate: You will be logged out and redirected to the Welcome screen
  • Data removal: All your activities, goals, folders, friends, and profile data are permanently deleted
  • No recovery: Your account and all associated data cannot be recovered
  • New account: You can create a new account anytime using the same email address

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I recover my account after deletion?

A: No, account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data (activities, goals, folders, friends) will be permanently removed.

Q: How long does the deletion process take?

A: The deletion process takes 10-30 seconds. You'll see a loading indicator and then be redirected to the Welcome screen.

Q: Can I create a new account with the same email?

A: Yes, you can create a new account with the same email address after your previous account is deleted.

Q: What if I can't find the "Delete Account" button?

A: Make sure you're in the Edit Profile screen (not the main Profile screen). The "Delete Account" button is at the bottom, below the "Sign Out" button.

Q: What if the deletion process fails?

A: If you see an error message, try again. If the problem persists, contact our support team at princess.zhirova@gmail.com.
